About Us

TechSlate Limited's vision is to establish a mentor-driven digital platform program that advances careers in the IT industry. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive destination platform for individuals seeking to enhance their competencies in line with their career aspirations. Our program allows users to learn at their own pace and time, empowering them with applied learning that enhances their competitiveness in the job market and workplace. Our platform aims to improve career progression opportunities and increase the chances of securing new job positions or switching careers.

RUN PROGRAM AS AGILE

We run each of our learning program as an Agile Program. Meaning all the Agile Ceremonies are conducted like Sprints, Sprint Planning, Retrospective Meetings, Standup meetings

DIGITAL CONTENT

Our content is extremly well designed Digital Content with good real time examples, animations and live demos. Each session follows with a Quiz to assess your understanding, also a coding exercise to make you comfortable with what you have learnt. Ofcourse, for any questions/clarfications you have a mentor allocated !

INTERVIEW READY

Making you interview ready is one of our main resposibility. Through out our program, you will learn to prepare your CV, interview questions on each topic, understanding job market, applying right jobs, talking to recruiters etc

JOB READY

The final stage is Job Ready, after our program completion, we help you with case studies, projects which you can pick and understand them more. You can start working on those projects to become job ready and we support you during this period
