Our end-to-end staffing services offer access to top skilled on-demand developers, providing your organization with a winning workforce that meets your unique business requirements.

At Techslate, we are a team of dedicated professionals who strive to deliver exceptional IT consulting services to our clients. We believe that our success is driven by the talented individuals who make up our team. As a result, we are always on the lookout for skilled and motivated individuals to join us and contribute to our continued growth. We offer a dynamic and fast-paced work environment that values innovation, creativity, and collaboration. Our team members have the opportunity to work with a diverse group of clients from various industries and gain exposure to the latest technologies and trends in the IT industry. If you are a passionate and driven individual who is looking for a challenging and rewarding career in IT consulting, we invite you to explore the opportunities available at Techslate. We are committed to providing our team members with the resources, training, and support they need to succeed in their roles and advance their careers.

Temporary Hiring:

TechSlate's temporary hiring services are designed to provide our clients with the flexibility they need to meet their staffing needs. Whether it's covering for absent employees, filling temporary projects, or meeting seasonal demand, our team of dedicated professionals is committed to providing result-oriented staffing solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Direct Hire:

Our Direct Hire staffing services are aimed at helping our clients find the right talent for long-term commitments. We understand the importance of finding qualified employees who not only possess the required skills but also fit in with your organization's culture and vision. That's why our team engages with clients to understand their specific requirements and searches for the best candidates to fill those roles.

Temp to Hire:

At TechSlate, we understand that sometimes the decision between temporary or permanent staffing can be unclear. That's why we offer Temp-to-Hire services, which give our clients the flexibility to contract qualified staff for a predetermined period while still having the option to convert them to permanent employees if desired.

Offshore Staffing:

In today's economic climate, outsourcing has become a popular option for many enterprises looking to increase profitability and focus on core processes. At TechSlate, we offer collaborative offshore staffing that ensures our clients get a team of skilled and experienced professionals who work exclusively as their offshore unit. Our offshore staff are equipped to work in close alignment with your project schedules, requirements, and business hours, delivering exceptional value and quality.

On-Site Staffing:

TechSlate's on-site staffing services are designed to provide our clients with the flexibility they need to meet their staffing needs. Our team of dedicated professionals is committed to providing result-oriented staffing solutions tailored to your specific requirements. We work closely with our clients to understand their specific needs and provide the best possible staffing solutions to meet those needs.
